-open the index.html file in your browser(chrome,firefox)

-select your excel files by clicking the choose file button in the top right corner

-draw shapes(circle,rectangle,polygon) on the google maps by selecting the shape from the drawing tools
 which are shown the google maps

-after drawing the shapes click on the 'Pins 1' or 'Pins 2' checkbox 

-then all the markers which falls within the shapes are shown on the google maps along with their list

-click on the marker to reveal its ID,Latitude,Longitude

-unchecking the checkbox will remove all of that markers
